Welcome to ShareFridge

This is the perfect site/app to note about you bought for your home and fridge. You can note down what you bought and share it with others living in your household.

What to do:

  • Create a Fridge
  • Open the newly created fridge and click on Edit/Invite and generate an invite Link
  • Send the invite link to other members of your household. They must open that link to join.
  • Create entries called expenses about what you bought.
  • The app takes track of what was bought and who spent how much money. You can use this to achieve a fair division of expenses among all members.

Who operates this app/site?

I am a Software developer that shares a flat. We used an app to note down who bought what/what it cost. But the app had a lot of advertisement and aggressive pop ups. So I decided to do my own app that covers our use case. Free to use and Open Source.
